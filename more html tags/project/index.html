Rice and Stew

Ingredients Details Steps Required
4 cups rice
20 fresh tomatoes big
5 cup vegetable oil
10 fresh pepper big
2 big onion bulb
3 tablespoon curry
7 cube maggi
1 tablespoon thyme
3 pieces ginger medium
6 cloves garlic
300 g chicken meat

1. Wash tomatoes, pepper, ginger, garlic and one onion blend.
2. Wash and chop onion thinly.
3. Get a clean pot, wash meat put in the pot spice with 4 cube maggi, some part of chop onion, thyme and little salt.
4. Place on heat add little water boil for 15min.
5. Place pan on heat add vegetable oil allow to heat for 5min, add boiled chicken meat, fry little by little until it finishes.
6. Use the same oil for frying to make stew. Reduce 2 cup veg oil place on heat allow to heat for 4 min.
7. Add the remaining onions and the blended ingredient stir and allow to fry untill the water dries up oil will be seen at the surface.
8. Add the meat stock, maggi, salt, curry and stir if stew is to thick add little water allow to boil for 10min.
9. Stew is ready.
10. Get a clean pot, place on heat, add water and the rice, allow to boil for 20min parboiling.
11. Wash and drain rice, put back on heat with little salt and water, allow to boil untill the water is dried for 20 more min.
12. Rice and stew is ready serve hot so so sumptious you can push it down with any soft drinks.

Boli

Ingredients Details Steps Required
Plantain
Fish
Tattase and rodo
Onion
Salt
Buillion cube
Palm oil
Vegetable oil

1. Wash and peel plantain. Set on your charcoal grill. Allow to roast on one side for about 5 minutes. Flip the plantain to roast reverse side. Allow to roast properly.
2. Cut and wash fish. Season with salt, buillion cube, fish seasoning. Heat vegetable oil. Deep fry fish in hot vegetable oil.
3. Blend tattase, rodo and onion. Bleach palm oil. Add blended pepper and onion. Season to taste with salt and buillion cube.
4. Scrape darkened part of plantain, slice in desired sizes. Serve alongside fried fish and pepper sauce.

Eba and Vegetable Soup

Ingredients Details Steps Required
1 big bunch ugu leaves
1 small bunch water leaves
fresh pepper
2 cooking spoon of red oil
dry fish
cow tail
medium onion
Seasoning and salt
crayfish

1. Slice the vegetables after washing Par-boil the cowtail and dry fish, add salt and seasoning until less stock.
2. Roughly blend the pepper and onion and add to the parboiled meat and fish and allow to boil for a minute. Add the sliced vegetables and crayfish then let boil for one minute.
3. Add Palm oil and taste for seasoning and leave to boil for 3 minutes. Done!

Beans

5 cups beans
I tuber of yam
Palm oil
Crayfish, crayfish, onion and fresh pepper
Seasoning cube,
salt

1 Prepare your ingredients
2 In a clean pot, add your cleaned beans and cook till it’s soft(used steaming method)…. Sieve out the water.
3 In the dried pot, add your palm oil and fry your onion, pepper and crayfish….. then add a little amount of water. Then go in with your salt and seasoning cube and bring to boil.
4 Pour the beans into the boiling base and then add the washed diced yam…. Cover the pot and allow cook.
5 Stir and adjust the seasoning if necessary… The off your heat source
6 Your food is ready to be served.
